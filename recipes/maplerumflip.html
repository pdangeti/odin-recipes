Maple Rum Flip

A classic flip cocktail shakes together liquor (or fortified wine) with sugar and a whole egg. This fall version uses rum, maple, rosemary, and nutmeg.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Rum (such as Appleton Estate)
  • 0.5 oz orange liqueur
  • 0.5 oz pure maple syrup
  • 1 pasteurized whole egg
  • ice as needed
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • freshly grated fresh nutmeg, for garnish

Directions

  1. Combine rum, orange liqueur, maple syrup, and egg in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Cover and shake until pressure builds and mixture starts to emulsify, about 45 seconds.
  3. Add ice and rosemary to shaker. Cover and shake until outside of shaker is frosted, about 30 seconds.
  4. Strain into a glass. Garnish with nutmeg.
